Length Contraction

When a body is moving at velocity v relative to a stationary observer its length measured is contracted in the direction of the motion by a factor of γ, that is a body's length is measured to be greatest when it is at rest relative to the observer. Note on Length contraction, the length is only contracted in the direction of the relative motion.
